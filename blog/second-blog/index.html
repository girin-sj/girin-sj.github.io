The importance of humanities

My second blog, Ted, review of watching it

Published on Jan 01, 0001

Reading time: 1 minutes.

Introduction

Some adults often say “Recently, math is very important.” or “How about studying science like physics and chemistry?”. In addition, some students say “I don’t have to study Korean or Chinese because math and science are more necessary to me.” However, I think it is wrong to regard humanities as unimportant study like Eric.

First Reason

First, the reason why humanities is important is because it determines the value of technology. If we focus on technology development without humanities, it can be fatal to people. For example, Nobel made dynamite without considering the negative effects on people. At first, the dynamite was beneficial for road construction and excavation work, but it was also used in wars that killed many people. So he felt guilty for inventing dynamite.

Second Reason

Also, humanities offers a variety of ideas about what people need. Jeff was a bartender who majored in philosophy. So he understood people well and knew what customers wanted. After meeting him, Eric not only hired people with STEM skills, but also people with humanities knowledge. Then they communicated with each other and came up with good ideas for the success of their business.

Conclusion

In conclusion, humanities is essential to our lives. Nevertheless, math and science are so emphasized these days that people don’t know the necessity of humanities. Therefore, we try to know importance of humanities and need the power to accept that.
